ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT ARE THE MAIN UPGRADES OF MAVIC 3 OVER PREVIOUS GENERATIONS?+

Mavic 3 features a dual-camera system, upgraded omnidirectional obstacle sensing, 46-minute max flight time, and O3+ transmission system providing up to 15km range.

HOW DOES THE DUAL-CAMERA SYSTEM WORK IN FLIGHT?+

You can seamlessly switch between the Hasselblad camera for wide shots and the Tele camera for 28x hybrid zoom, all while recording continuously.

WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN APAS 4.0 AND APAS 5.0?+

APAS 5.0 utilizes enhanced algorithms to detect and bypass obstacles faster and more reliably in complex environments.

DO I HAVE TO USE THE RC PRO OR CAN I USE MY SMARTPHONE?+

You can use the standard RC-N1 controller paired with your smartphone, but the RC Pro offers a built-in ultra-bright screen for professional use.
